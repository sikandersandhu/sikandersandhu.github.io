Welcome to Art Works Magic!

At Art Works Magic, we help children unlock their full potential through creative, hands-on activities tailored to their unique needs. Our sessions combine art, crafts, and developmental games in a safe, nurturing environment to support therapy goals, boost essential skills, and foster confidence. With a focus on flexible thinking, healthy risk-taking, and positive growth, we make every session fun, inspiring, and impactful, helping children thrive while creating meaningful connections.

Our Mission

Let’s Create Magic Together!

At the heart of our programs is a simple yet powerful belief: arts, crafts, and movement have the unique ability to ground us in the present moment. These creative tools inspire joy, foster exploration, and make learning an enjoyable journey. Through fun and hands-on experiences, we unlock the potential for growth and discovery.

Together with our participants, we create customised, multi-sensory learning experiences that ignite imagination, nurture creativity, and provide support and empowerment throughout their unique developmental journeys.

Frequently Asked Questions

No, this is not an art therapy service. Instead, we provide therapy support to your child between their sessions with professionals such as occupational therapists, psychologists, and others. While we use engaging mediums like art, crafts, games, and movement to help your child work toward their developmental goals, our services are not classified as art therapy. Our focus is on complementing your child’s existing therapy plan in a fun, creative, and supportive way.

The sessions are suitable for ages 4 years and up, providing engaging and age-appropriate support tailored to their developmental needs and interests.
